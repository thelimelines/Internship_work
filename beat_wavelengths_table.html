Beat wavelengths for LPmn modes

The propagation constants, \( \beta_{mn} \), for different modes in a waveguide are calculated based on the cut-off wave number \( k_c \) and the wave number in free space \( k \) using the formula:

\[ \beta_{mn} = \sqrt{k^2 - k_{c_{mn}}^2} \]

Where \( k_c \) is given by:

\[ k_{c_{mn}} = \frac{{\text{{zero of the Bessel function for mode }} mn}}{a} \]

Given two different modes with propagation constants \( \beta_{m1n1} \) and \( \beta_{m2n2} \), the beat wavelength between these modes is given by:

\[ \lambda_B = \frac{2 \pi}{|\beta_{m1n1} - \beta_{m2n2}|} \]
